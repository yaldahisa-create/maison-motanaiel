Return / Exchange Policy

At MotanielOfficial, we understand that sometimes a suit might not be the perfect fit or style. This page outlines our policies and procedures for returns and exchanges to ensure a smooth and satisfactory experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we receive regarding returns and exchanges. We hope this information clarifies our policies and helps you understand the process better.

What are the usual reasons someone wants to return or exchange a suit?

  • Fit Issues: Suit feels too tight or too loose, jacket sleeves or pants length not right, or overall silhouette doesn’t match expectations.
  • Color or Style Mismatch: The shade looks different in person than online, or doesn’t match the occasion or personal style once tried on.
  • Fabric or Quality Concerns: Material feels different than expected (too heavy, too thin, not breathable), or stitching, lining, or finish doesn’t meet expectations.
  • Wrong Item Received: Incorrect size, color, or style shipped by mistake.
  • Event Change / No Longer Needed: The event (wedding, gala, job interview) was canceled or plans changed.
  • Price Sensitivity: Customer finds a better deal elsewhere after purchase.

What condition should the suit be in for a return or exchange?

Due to the nature of our process and the garment being made with specific measurements, all custom-made or altered garments are usually non-refundable and non-exchangeable.

Instead, we offer in-house credit (terms & conditions apply).

The in-house credit is provided in the form of a MotanielOfficial e-gift card, which excludes original shipping costs.

The e-gift card will be emailed to your email address and will include instructions on how to use the in-house credit.

How do customers start a return or exchange?

  • 1. Contact Us First: Email us or DM us on Instagram within 10 days of receiving your order. Include the item(s) and reason for return.
  • 2. Wait for Approval: Our team will confirm if the item is eligible and provide return instructions. Items sent without approval will not be accepted.

How long do customers have to request a return?

At MotanielOfficial, customers have a 10-day window from the date of delivery to request in-house credit.

Terms and conditions apply.

Need Assistance?

If you have any further questions or need assistance with a return or exchange, please contact our team. We're here to help.
