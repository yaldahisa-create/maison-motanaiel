Returns & Exchanges

At MotanielOfficial, we understand that sometimes a garment may not be the perfect fit or style. This page explains our returns and exchange process to ensure clarity and care.

Policy Information

Return & Exchange Guidance

Below are some of the most common questions we receive regarding returns, exchanges, and in-house credit. We hope this page provides clarity and helps guide your next step.

What are the usual reasons someone wants to return or exchange a suit?

  1. Fit Issues: The suit feels too tight or too loose, jacket sleeves or trouser length are not right, or the overall silhouette does not match expectations.
  2. Color or Style Mismatch: The shade appears different in person than online, or it no longer matches the occasion or personal style after trying it on.
  3. Fabric or Quality Concerns: The material feels different than expected, such as too heavy, too thin, or not breathable enough, or the stitching, lining, or finish does not meet expectations.
  4. Wrong Item Received: The incorrect size, color, or style was shipped by mistake.
  5. Event Change / No Longer Needed: The event, such as a wedding, gala, or interview, was canceled or plans changed.
  6. Price Sensitivity: A customer finds a better deal elsewhere after purchase.

What condition should the garment be in for a return or exchange to be accepted?

Due to the nature of our process and the fact that garments are often made with specific measurements, all custom-made or altered garments are usually non-refundable and non-exchangeable.

Instead, we offer in-house credit where applicable. Terms and conditions apply.

In-house credit is provided in the form of a MotanielOfficial e-gift card, which excludes original shipping costs.

The e-gift card will be sent to your email address and will include instructions on how to use your in-house credit.

What are the steps a customer needs to take to start a return or exchange?

  1. Contact Us First: Email us or send us a direct message on Instagram within 10 days of receiving your order. Include the item(s) you wish to return and the reason for the request.
  2. Wait for Approval: Our team will confirm whether your item is eligible and provide next-step instructions. Items sent without prior approval will not be accepted.

How long do customers typically have to request a return or exchange?

At MotanielOfficial, a 10-day window is allowed to request in-house credit from the date of delivery.

Terms and conditions apply.
