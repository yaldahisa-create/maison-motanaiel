Maison Motaniel Consultation

A Private Tailoring Experience

Every Maison Motaniel garment begins with a conversation. Our consultation process is designed to understand your occasion, your desired fit, your style direction, and the details that will shape the final piece.

What To Expect

An experience shaped with care, detail, and intention.

The consultation is where the Maison Motaniel experience begins. This is the stage where we understand your vision, discuss your occasion, and guide the direction of the garment through thoughtful conversation.


Whether you are preparing for a wedding, a special event, a statement look, or a bespoke everyday garment, this process allows us to shape a piece that feels personal, refined, and aligned with your presence.

The Consultation Journey

How the experience unfolds.

01. Initial Inquiry

Share your occasion, desired timeline, and the type of garment you are seeking.

02. Style Direction

We discuss silhouette, aesthetic, inspiration, and the overall statement the garment should make.

03. Measurements & Fit

Measurements and fit considerations are taken into account to ensure the garment is shaped with precision.

04. Fabric & Detail

Interior details, finishing, lining direction, and garment personality are discussed with care.

05. Final Direction

The final path for your garment is confirmed so the making process can begin with clarity.

06. Ongoing Guidance

Throughout the process, Maison Motaniel remains attentive to the experience and presentation of the final piece.

Who This Is For

Clients seeking a tailored experience.

Maison Motaniel consultations are ideal for clients seeking garments for weddings, milestone occasions, celebrations, bespoke suiting, statement tailoring, women’s tailoring, and signature garment development.


If you value intention, structure, elegance, and personal detail, this process is designed for you.

Before You Reach Out

Helpful information to prepare.

To begin smoothly, it helps to have your event date, general garment direction, inspiration references, and timeline in mind before contacting Maison Motaniel.

Occasion

Wedding, formal event, celebration, statement garment, or bespoke wardrobe need.

Timeline

Your intended wear date and any important deadlines connected to the garment.

Inspiration

Any visual references, silhouettes, colors, or details you would like the house to understand.
