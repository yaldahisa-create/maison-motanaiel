The Experience

A private process designed around you.

The Maison Motaniel consultation is where the tailoring journey begins. It is an opportunity to discuss your occasion, personal style, preferred fit, and the details that will define the final garment.


Whether the piece is for a wedding, a celebration, a statement look, or a refined wardrobe addition, the consultation ensures that every decision is intentional and aligned with your vision.

What The Consultation Covers

A tailored conversation before the garment is created.

Occasion & Direction

Discuss the purpose of the garment, the tone of the look, and the overall style direction you want to achieve.

Fit & Personal Style

Refine the silhouette, structure, and overall feel of the garment so it reflects your identity and preferences.

Fabric & Detail

Explore material direction, interior customization, and the signature details that will shape the final piece.

The Tailoring Flow

From conversation to final garment.

01. Consultation

A private discussion to understand the occasion, garment vision, and personal direction.

02. Design Planning

The fit, styling details, and customization choices are shaped into a refined tailoring direction.

03. Bespoke Creation

The garment is crafted with precision, intention, and finishing details that reflect the Maison Motaniel house.
