About Maison Motaniel

The House of Motaniel

A bespoke tailoring house creating garments with story, precision, and presence for men and women who value elegance and individuality.

Who We Are

Crafted with intention, tailored with meaning.

Maison Motaniel is a bespoke tailoring house dedicated to creating refined garments through private consultation, thoughtful craftsmanship, and elevated personal style.


Each piece is designed with purpose — from silhouette and structure to the details hidden inside — allowing every garment to reflect the identity, taste, and story of the person wearing it.

Our Philosophy

Tailoring beyond the garment.

Maison Motaniel believes that tailoring is not simply about clothing. It is about presence, confidence, memory, and personal expression.

Personal Expression

Every garment is crafted to reflect the wearer’s individuality and vision.

Precision Craft

Structure, fit, and detail are approached with care to ensure an elevated finish.

Meaningful Detail

Interior linings and signature details transform garments into personal stories.

The Experience

A house built on consultation and craftsmanship.

Every Maison Motaniel piece begins with conversation. Through consultation, the house explores the occasion, style direction, fit preferences, and special details that shape the final garment.


This process allows each piece to feel intentional, polished, and deeply personal — whether created for a wedding, a celebration, a statement moment, or everyday distinction.
