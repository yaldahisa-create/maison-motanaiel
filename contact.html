About The House

A House Of Bespoke Tailoring

Maison Motaniel creates garments that combine tailoring, identity, and elevated craftsmanship. The house is built around personal consultation, refined structure, and a belief that clothing should carry story, confidence, and timeless presence.

Who We Are

Tailoring designed for presence, memory, and distinction.

Maison Motaniel serves clients seeking garments that feel personal, polished, and deeply considered. From weddings and statement suits to women’s bespoke and signature details, every piece is developed through a process that values fit, elegance, and lasting impression.


The house is not simply about clothing. It is about creating garments that reflect identity and elevate the wearer through craftsmanship, consultation, and intentional detail.
