About The House

Custom tailoring with story, presence, and precision.

Maison Motaniel is a consultation-based tailoring house creating bespoke garments for men and women who value individuality, elegance, and a precise fit. Each piece is crafted with intention — from silhouette and structure to signature interior details that reflect the client’s story.

For Men

Wedding suits, formalwear, statement tailoring, and custom looks designed to reflect confidence and distinction.

For Women

Power suits, elegant tailoring, event looks, and bespoke garments made for women who lead with presence.

Signature Personalization

Custom linings with memories, scripture, and meaningful details that make every garment personal and unforgettable.
